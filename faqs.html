Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

We are open from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday through Friday, and from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekends.

Our coffee shop is located at 123 Brew Lane, Coffee City, CA 90210.

Menu and Dietary Options

Yes, we have a variety of vegan options including almond milk, soy milk, oat milk, and a selection of vegan pastries.

Absolutely! You can customize your drink with different milk options, syrups, and espresso shots.

Loyalty Program

Yes, we have a loyalty program where you can earn points for every purchase. After earning a certain number of points, you can redeem them for free drinks or discounts.

You can sign up for our loyalty program at the counter or through our mobile app.
Special Offers and Events

Yes, we have daily specials that vary each day. Check our menu board or ask a barista for today's special.

Yes, we host regular coffee tasting events, barista workshops, and live music nights. Follow us on social media or check our website for upcoming events.
Ordering and Delivery

Yes, you can place an order through our website or our mobile app for pickup or delivery.

Yes, we offer delivery through various delivery partners like Uber Eats, Grubhub, and DoorDash.
Sustainability

Yes, we are committed to sustainability. We use compostable cups, source our coffee beans from fair trade suppliers, and offer discounts for customers who bring their own reusable cups.

Yes, we have recycling bins in our shop for paper cups, and we encourage customers to use them.
